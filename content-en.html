BigChanger

BigChanger is an international project focused on the development of immersive technologies, tourism, and media. The company is building a new type of entertainment and technology environment where architecture, cinema, digital media, and advanced audiovisual systems are integrated into one unified experience. The goal of the project is to create a destination where visitors do not simply watch content but become part of it.

BigChanger HoloCinema Dome

The core of the project is the BigChanger HoloCinema Dome — a next-generation immersive cinema environment. Unlike traditional cinemas, the audience is placed inside the visual and audio space of the film. Visitors watch the show while lying on specially designed lounge platforms arranged in a grid layout. The entire dome surface becomes a projection environment combined with multi-channel spatial audio and holographic elements, creating a fully immersive cinematic experience. The complex also includes two hotels, public recreation areas, a technology museum, and infrastructure designed to host international events, festivals, and creative industry gatherings.
